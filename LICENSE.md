## 🔧 Troubleshooting & Common Issues

### The overlay doesn't appear after execution
- Ensure your loader is fully up to date. Outdated loaders often fail silently.
- Re-attach to the game process and execute again.
- Restart the popular title completely before retrying.

### Auto parry feels delayed or inconsistent
- Check your `parry_delay_ms` setting and lower it if needed.
- Confirm your internet latency is below 100ms. High ping degrades timing accuracy.
- Close background applications consuming CPU cycles.

### Sword unlocks reset after rejoining
- Make sure `unlock_all_swords` is set to `true` in your config file.
- Re-execute the script after joining a new server — unlocks are session-based.

### Android loader crashes on launch
- Clear the loader's cache via **Settings → Apps → [Loader Name] → Clear Cache**.
- Verify your Android version is 10 or above.
- Reinstall the loader from a fresh APK download.

### Script doesn't load from paste site
- Copy the raw text, not the formatted page. Look for a **"Raw"** button on the paste site.
- Check that you haven't accidentally included extra whitespace or line breaks at the top of the pasted code.
- Some paste sites expire links after a set period — verify the link is still active.

### macOS Gatekeeper blocks the loader
- Go to **System Settings → Privacy & Security** and click **"Open Anyway"** next to the blocked application.
- Alternatively, run `xattr -cr /path/to/loader.app` in Terminal to strip quarantine flags.
